Phi-4 Medical VQA Fine-tuning

Fine-tuned Microsoft Phi-4 Vision (32K-context multimodal LLM) on VQA-RAD medical imaging data using LoRA (r=16, α=32) on all attention layers (q/k/v/o). Multi-GPU DDP training with BF16 precision, 4-step gradient accumulation, and early stopping. Evaluation covers exact-match, BLEU/ROUGE, and clinical yes/no binary accuracy with WandB tracking.

Rerender A Video — Zero-Shot Video Translation

Zero-shot text-guided video-to-video framework on ControlNet (HED/Canny) and Stable Diffusion. Enforces temporal consistency via GMFlow optical flow with hierarchical cross-frame constraints on shape, texture, and color. Extended with NinaSR super-resolution, MIRNet low-light enhancement, AudioLDM audio adaptation, and a Gradio UI with LoRA-based style customization.
